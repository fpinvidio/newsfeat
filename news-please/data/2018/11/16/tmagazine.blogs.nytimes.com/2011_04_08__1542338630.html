Glass Menagerie

Women's Fashion
By Chelsea Zalopany
April 8, 2011 11:00 am April 8, 2011 11:00 am
Jens MortensenBright eyes Ten Thousand Things flower necklace, $265. Go to tenthousandthingsnyc.com or ylang23.com.

When the designer Gregory Parkinson approached the specialty jeweler Ten Thousand Things to create accessories for his spring 2010 collection, his only request was “riots of color.” The result is a series of coin-size glass enamel talismans, which have since become a permanent fixture at Ten Thousand Things’ meatpacking district studio/boutique. “As you stack them, it becomes like a composition of sorts,” says David Rees of Ten Thousand Things. He and his partner, Ron Anderson, create folksy bijoux by fastening the swirling, multicolored pendants onto necklaces. “It’s like we’re making stones instead of finding stones, and how fun is that?”
