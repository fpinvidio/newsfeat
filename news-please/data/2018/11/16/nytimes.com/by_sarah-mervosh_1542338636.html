    Sarah Mervosh

    Sarah Mervosh covers breaking news for The New York Times. She was previously an investigative reporter at The Dallas Morning News. Her background is primarily in covering criminal justice and legal issues, but she has written about everything from dangerous psychiatric hospitals to a celebrity feline’s appearance at a Texas cat cafe.
