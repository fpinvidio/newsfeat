    The police said the attacker, identified as Sayfullo Saipov, rented the truck at 2:06 p.m. from a Home Depot in Passaic, N.J., close to his home in Paterson. The truck crossed the George Washington Bridge into Manhattan at 2:43 p.m. and turned south onto the West Side Highway.

    The truck entered the bike path at 3:04 p.m. at Houston Street and traveled at high speed, appearing to target bikers and pedestrians. The police said the truck collided with a school bus four minutes later at Chambers Street.

    The attack left a terrible trail of mangled bicycles and victims thrown aside. A video posted on Twitter showed a man with objects in his hands racing around the scene. Witnesses said he was yelling. Another video showed the police later standing over a man lying in the street.

    The police said the attacker had a pellet gun and a paintball gun.
