    Credit Scores

    News about Credit Scores, including commentary and archival articles published in The New York Times. More

    Because of the economic turmoil, consumers are having an increasingly hard time getting loans. How lenders view your creditworthiness is critical to determining how you live your life. Luckily, your credit score is something you can control. It's a good idea to focus on improving it, and this section provides advice on how to do that.
