    Issues Where Trump’s Cabinet Picks and Most Americans Differ

    By K.K. REBECCA LAI DEC. 9, 2016

    Many of the people selected by President-elect Donald J. Trump for his cabinet have views that are at odds with those of most Americans. And in some cases, their perspectives also clash with those of most people who identify as Republicans. Here are some examples.
