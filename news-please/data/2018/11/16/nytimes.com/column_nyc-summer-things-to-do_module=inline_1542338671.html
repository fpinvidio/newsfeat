    Summer: The Best Summer Plans for New York City

    The best of what to see and do in New York City each summer week, and where to eat and drink nearby.

    Highlights

    1. Things to Do in New York This Weekend: Sharks in Brooklyn? You Better Believe It.

      It’s time to meet your marine neighbors at the New York Aquarium, and have some good, old-fashioned fun at the Bronx Brewery.

      By MARGOT BOYER-DRY and TEJAL RAO

    2. Things to Do: Cold Spring by Kayak and Living History on the Lower East Side

      Just a bit north of the city, a castle and an estate, kayaking and hiking; plus a look at life before modern amenities, at the Tenement Museum.

      By MARGOT BOYER-DRY and TEJAL RAO

      1. Things to Do in N.Y.C. This Weekend

        Nine scoops of ice cream in one day? Why not? Also, free jazz and a visit to Manhattan’s oldest house.

        By MARGOT BOYER-DRY and TEJAL RAO

      2. Things to Do in N.Y.C. This Weekend

        Let’s hit the last Uptown Bounce of the season, and head inside for Summer Shorts.

        By MARGOT BOYER-DRY and TEJAL RAO

    1. Things to do This Weekend: Free Arts in Parks!

      Noname headlines BRIC Celebrate Brooklyn on Friday, and it’s the second year of the INSITU dance festival in Queens.

      By MARGOT BOYER-DRY and TEJAL RAO

    2. Things to Do This Weekend: A War Film on a Flight Deck and a New Old Theater

      Plus free workouts, free concerts and a group surf session in the Rockaways.

      By MARGOT BOYER-DRY and TEJAL RAO

    3. Free Things to Do in New York: Museums and More, Thanks to Your Library Card

      For kids, artists or the history buffs, we share the cultural institutions now free to holders of Manhattan, Queens and Brooklyn library cards.

      By MARGOT BOYER-DRY

    4. Things to Do in N.Y.C.: Astoria for a Pool Day, and Open-Mic Night Plus Dumplings in Chinatown

      A how-to for one of the best city pools, and a doozy of a cultural event in Chinatown.

      By MARGOT BOYER-DRY and TEJAL RAO

    5. Free (or Cheap) Things to Do in N.Y.C.: How to Get to the Beach

      We heard you wanted more free activities and events from us. All right then!

      By MARGOT BOYER-DRY and TEJAL RAO

    1. What to Do on the 4th of July in N.Y.C.

      With lots of free events.

      By MARGOT BOYER-DRY and TEJAL RAO

    2. Things to Do in N.Y.C. This Weekend: MoMA PS1 and Quad Cinema

      If you happen to be one of those New Yorkers who appreciates good multitasking, have we got plans for you.

      By MARGOT BOYER-DRY and TEJAL RAO

    3. Fun Things to Do This Weekend: Pride, Summer Solstice and Sensory Overload in Brighton Beach

      This week we’ve got a lot to celebrate: Pride, of course, but also the first official week of summer. Join us!

      By MARGOT BOYER-DRY and TEJAL RAO

    4. Things to Do in New York: History, Funk and the Queens Night Market

      We’re living history this week, leaning into the past with visits to our city’s many founders and reliving it at a show by funk legends. Join us!

      By MARGOT BOYER-DRY and TEJAL RAO

    5. Things to Do in New York: Shakespeare (Drunk or in the Park)

      This week’s Summer column is all about the Bard.

      By MARGOT BOYER-DRY and TEJAL RAO
