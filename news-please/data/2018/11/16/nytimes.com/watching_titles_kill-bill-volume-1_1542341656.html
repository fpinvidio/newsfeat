NOTEWORTHY

Kill Bill Volume 1

2003• 1 hour and 50 minutes• Uma Thurman, Lucy Liu, Vivica A. Fox, Michael Madsen and Daryl Hannah• Directed by Quentin Tarantino

After waking from a coma, an erstwhile assassin seeks revenge against the former colleagues who tried to kill her.

if you like over-the-top action movies with strong female characters. Channeling western, martial-arts and exploitation movies of the past, director Quentin Tarantino creates an action-fueled revenge story that, true to his style, fractures the story’s timeline. Uma Thurman’s character, the Bride, fights to the death against dozens of combatants, and there aren’t many breaks between violent sequences.
