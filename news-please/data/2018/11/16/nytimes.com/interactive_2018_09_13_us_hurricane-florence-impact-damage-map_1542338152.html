    Mapping Florence’s Impact: Rainfall, Power Outages, and Wind

    UPDATED SEPT. 18, 2018

    Record amounts of rain have fallen in North Carolina since the outer edges of Hurricane Florence first reached the coast. The hurricane was downgraded to a tropical storm after it made landfall on Friday, but it continued to move slowly inland, dumping more rain on the Carolinas. For the latest updates on the storm, see our live briefing.
