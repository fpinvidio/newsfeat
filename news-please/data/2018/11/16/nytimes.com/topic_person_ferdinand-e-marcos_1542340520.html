''Dad, we've got to hide, because they are arresting us,'' Mr. Olegario said Monday, quoting his son, a member of the opposition to Mr. Marcos. ''I just told him, 'Be careful, take care of yourself.''' But like many others during Mr. Marcos's 20 years in power, he said, his son ''just disappeared.''
