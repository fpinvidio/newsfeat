    Lucas Peterson

    Lucas Peterson is the Frugal Traveler columnist for The New York Times. He is a James Beard Award nominee for his work in Lucky Peach magazine and has written for GQ, Eater, LA Weekly and Food Republic. His video series for Eater, "Dining on a Dime," is now in its 11th season. He's spent years working, studying, and volunteering in countries around the world.
