    April 10, 2009 9:00 am April 10, 2009 9:00 am

    For a time on Thursday, President Obama seemed to be playing a mortgage broker on television, urging Americans not to miss out on rock-bottom refinancing rates, Jeff Zeleny writes in a Washington Memo.
