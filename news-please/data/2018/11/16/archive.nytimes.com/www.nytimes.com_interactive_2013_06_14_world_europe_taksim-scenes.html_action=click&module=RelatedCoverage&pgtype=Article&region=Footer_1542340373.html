Inside Istanbul’s Taksim Square

The Turkish prime minister, Recep Tayyip Erdogan, gave an ultimatum this week for protesters to evacuate Taksim Square in Istanbul. The area has been the site of clashes between the police and people demonstrating against a renovation that would radically change the square’s layout.
•
Drag this image
Ben Solomon for The New York Times
Protesters have camped for more than a week at Gezi Park, adjacent to Taksim Square. They have made a small tent city equipped with a medical clinic, places to buy food and even daily yoga classes.
Drag this image
Ed Ou for The New York Times
Protesters also built barricades in Taksim Square using debris gathered from previous days of confrontation. The renovation plan would replace the park area with a replica of an Ottoman-era barracks that occupied the site early in the 20th century.
