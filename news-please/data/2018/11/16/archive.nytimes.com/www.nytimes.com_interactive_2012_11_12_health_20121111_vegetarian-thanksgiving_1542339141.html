    Well’s Vegetarian Thanksgiving 2012

    Every fall, Well goes vegetarian for Thanksgiving, taking the meaty bird off the table to make room for a spectacular array of vegetarian soups, sides, main courses, salads and desserts. So get ready to save a turkey and savor the flavors of the fall harvest. Every day until Thanksgiving, we will add more vegetarian recipes for your holiday table.
