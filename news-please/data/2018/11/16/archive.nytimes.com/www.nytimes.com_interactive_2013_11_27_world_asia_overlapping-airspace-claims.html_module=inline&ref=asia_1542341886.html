Overlapping Airspace Claims in the East China Sea

On Nov. 23, China declared the right to monitor and request identification from aircraft flying above much of the East China Sea. China’s newly claimed airspace overlaps with similar claims by Japan, South Korea and Taiwan. South Korea expanded its zone on Dec. 8 in response to China’s claim. Related Article »
