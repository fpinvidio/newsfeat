How Minorities Have Fared in States With Affirmative Action Bans

The Supreme Court for the second time heard arguments on how race is used in admissions decisions by the University of Texas at Austin, with a majority of justices expressing doubts that the university's plan is constitutional. A ban on affirmative action could lead to fewer minority admissions, as it has in some states that have already outlawed it. Related Article »
