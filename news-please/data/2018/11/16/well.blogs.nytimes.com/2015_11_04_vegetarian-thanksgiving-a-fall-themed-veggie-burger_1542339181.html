    Vegetarian Thanksgiving: A Fall-Themed Veggie Burger

    By Tara Parker-Pope
    November 4, 2015 11:58 am November 4, 2015 11:58 am

    Bailey Rae, a food writer in Nashville, created a fall-themed veggie burger to star on her Thanksgiving table. The dish requires some prep time to bake, scoop and, well, squash, the acorn squash and caramelize the onions, but once those steps are out of the way, this is an easy dish to add to your Thanksgiving menu.

    We like this dish because it captures so many fall flavors in one bite.

    “I love acorn squash and love creating new ways to use it,” said Ms. Rae, founder of The Local Vegan food blog. “This recipe shouts fall and Thanksgiving with the cranberries, toasted nuts and squash. ”
