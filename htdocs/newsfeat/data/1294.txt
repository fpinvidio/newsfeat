MILAN — Never heard of Samuele Failli before? If not, you’re still likely to have seen a lot of his shoes.Mr. Failli, an Italian footwear designer, has spent much of the last 20 years designing for some of the biggest names in the luxury business, including Prada, Tom Ford, Saint Laurent and, most recently, Azzedine Alaïa.Now, the 40-year-old designer has decided to step out on his own, quietly introducing a collection with his own name during Milan Fashion Week.
