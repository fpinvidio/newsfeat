Evacuations are underway in coastal areas of North Carolina, South Carolina and Virginia, with more than a million residents preparing to leave ahead of Hurricane Florence.The Category 4 storm is expected to make landfall on Thursday evening. Meteorologists have issued warnings about the potential impact of high winds and life-threatening storm surges.Many residents have been through this before: Two years ago, thousands of coastal South Carolinians evacuated as Hurricane Matthew approached.
