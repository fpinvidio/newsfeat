There’s spice in those bottles, and some sunny heat. That brightness welcomes the tomatoes, herbs, eggplant, lamb and olives of the Southern Rhône region, to be sure, but it can also have more global aspirations.So I started with lamb, eggplant, tomatoes and herbs, then added chiles and curry seasonings to make a meatball ragout to serve over rice, evoking the south of India more than France. If you prefer beef to lamb, it’s a fine substitute.
