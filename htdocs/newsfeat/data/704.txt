When you think of finger foods, thoughts generally gravitate toward something small and wrapped in bacon. For a more elegant approach, roasted meat is the way to go. If you can get small lamb chops (teeny-tiny but not fussy), cover the bones in scallion ''sleeves” for a whimsical look and a practical touch. If your guests don’t mind, they’ll have a built-in utensil — something like a lamb lollipop.Featured in: Food; Here Comes The Chow.Learn: How to Grill
