PhotoLONDON — Latest advice dealing with the revolt of the Arabs, under the Shereef of Mecca, against Turkish rule, go to show that the disaffection is more widespread than first accounts indicated, and should the capture of the sacred cities of Mecca, Jeddah and Taif encourage the remainder of the Arabs throughout the Ottoman dominions to overthrow the Sultan’s authority, the Turkish Government will find itself faced with insurmountable difficulties, and German intrigue will have sustained a crushing blow. —The New York Herald, European Edition, June 24, 1916
