A simple French soup that works well regardless of which vegetable gets the emphasis.This is a simple French soup. If you want to vary the proportions of vegetables you can; it works well whether you emphasize the turnips, as I do here, the leeks or the potatoes. Turnips have a slightly bitter edge, and tarragon makes a lovely sweet garnish. Chives would also work.Featured in: Soup To Start.Learn: How to Make Soup
