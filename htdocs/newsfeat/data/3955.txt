June 21, 1995Manning, S.C.Macedonia Baptist ChurchFour former members of the Ku Klux Klan set fire to the church, one of several burned by arsonists in the mid-1990s. A fire was set the day before at the Mount Zion A.M.E. Church in Greeleyville, S.C. Macedonia Baptist was awarded $37.8 million in a decision against the Klan. A jury believed the Klan's rhetoric motivated the men to set the fire.The fire was one of dozens at predominantly black churches across the South that were investigated as arson.
