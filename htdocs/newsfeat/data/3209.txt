J.D. Biersdorfer has been answering technology questions for The New York Times — in print, on the web, in audio and in video — since 1998.She also writes the Sunday Book Review’s regular “Applied Reading” column on ebooks and literary apps, among other things. Outside The Times, Ms. Biersdorfer is the author or co-author of eight books on consumer-technology topics and co-hosts the independent Pop Tech Jam podcast.
