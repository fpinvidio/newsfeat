Dr. Sara Elizabeth Smolinski and Lin Zhao are to be married Oct. 1 at St. Robert of Newminster Parish in Ada, Mich. The Rev. Collin Mulhall, a Roman Catholic priest, is to perform the ceremony.The bride, who is 31 and taking her husband’s name, is an interventional radiology physician at Yale New Haven Hospital in New Haven, Conn. She graduated from Grand Valley State University in Allendale, Mich., and received a medical degree from Wayne State University.She is a daughter of Karen Smolinski and Patrick Smolinski, both of Belair, Mich. The bride’s father retired as an art teacher at Kalkaska Middle School in Kalkaska Mich., where her mother teaches English.The groom, also 31, is a product director in Boston, for Wayfair, an online home furnishings company. He graduated from Willamette University in Salem, Ore., and received an M.B.A. from Harvard.
