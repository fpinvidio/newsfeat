Otto F. Warmbier, the American college student sentenced to 15 years of prison and hard labor on Wednesday in North Korea, is one of about a dozen Americans arrested there in recent years. Some have been expelled quickly and others have been held as long as two years. They have been accused of crimes including illegal entry and seeking the overthrow of the Pyongyang government.
